RF IDeas WaveID Partner Program

The WaveID Partner Program is designed to strengthen the relationships between RF IDeas and our valued partners, while providing opportunities to increase profitability.

This program provides an easy and convenient way to work with RF IDeas and our joint customers and prospects to provide unique solutions that complement our partners’ offerings with RF IDeas’ broad portfolio of products and services.

The WaveID Program provides practical tools and services that will help partners market and sell their solutions to the same targeted industries. We understand the importance of developing strong relationships with companies, therefore, our partners receive the highest quality of service and support to ensure success.

To learn more about WaveID Partner Program and the program’s benefits, please read through the WaveID Partner Program Guide or email us at WaveID@RFIDeas.com
